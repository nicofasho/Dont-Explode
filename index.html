Avoid the Spoilers - 2019 Version McBoogaloo

This is a game that mirrors the struggle to avoid spoilers of popular movies, tv shows, and video games on Twitter, stream chats, and random day to day conversations. It's like playing Minesweeper. Avoid the mines like you avoid Chatty Kathy or Talkative Tim until you've seen that episode or movie twist for yourself. If not, you're gettin spoiled, good luck!
